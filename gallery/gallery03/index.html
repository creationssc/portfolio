--- layout: page title: "Rio" description: "From a multitude of physical and psychological sensations, a communicative channel was born for a journey focused on pain. Rio is an artistic intervention that represents the journey of a life in pain, doubt and constant search for an answer or solution. The inattentive contemplation of the observer only understands its apparently calm superficial beauty, but in itself it keeps a whole life of instability, analogously, the condition of living with an unseen pain. A mechanism used by many to cooperate with pain is laughter and comedy, so superficially “I laugh” in the same way that the river is calm and beautiful. Its pictorial component explores the representation of water channels that infiltrate and dominate the infrastructure, these hold the symbolism of all the circumstances and facets involved in pain, which converge, and result in a cathartic state, whether physical or psychological." active: gallery header-img: "img/g03-bg.jpg" album-title: "" images: - image_path: /gallery/archive/g03/1.jpg caption: University of Madeira mfp-title: copyright: - image_path: /gallery/archive/g03/2.jpg caption: University of Madeira copyright: - image_path: /gallery/archive/g03/3.jpg caption: University of Madeira copyright: - image_path: /gallery/archive/g03/4.jpg caption: University of Madeira mfp-title: copyright: - image_path: /gallery/archive/g03/5.jpg caption: University of Madeira copyright: ---
