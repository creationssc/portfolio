--- layout: page title: "InPorta" description: "InPorta is an artistic project that aims to respond to the view of the city of Funchal through a documental approach focused on the typical city doors, understanding it as a journey of knowledge and recognition. The city as a route holds a great symbolic value, it is the time spent on this pathway and the predisposition to spare it that cumulatively result in a relationship between the observer and the city itself. In this relationship, as in all, the journey is also the process of knowledge and recognition of a very specific identity. Funchal, as a city, has several elements that belong to its identity and I found it through its doors." active: gallery header-img: "img/g04-bg.jpg" album-title: "" images: - image_path: /gallery/archive/g07/bg1.jpg caption: mfp-title: copyright: ---
